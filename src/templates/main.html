Editor's letterDesign is a simple word: it is impossible to think of another that describes its meaning so well. Design is what makes your sofa comfortable and your clothes look on trend, but it could also save the planet if we find the right sustainable practice. In architecture, interiors, products, art, fashion and the groundbreaking innovations of experimental building materials, it is design that drives the world forward. So here is a special celebration of design from the Observer, a series of features covering everything from how to make a biobrick, to how to choose good-looking chairs. There are interviews with and columns by such design world leaders as Tadao Ando, Raf Simons, Tom Dixon, Jonathan Anderson and our own architecture critic Rowan Moore, and we let a group of kids test this year’s furniture trends. Enjoy Observer Design.Alice Fisher
